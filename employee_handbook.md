# Employee Handbook - TechCorp Inc.

## Company Overview
TechCorp Inc. is a leading software development company founded in 2015. We specialize in AI-powered solutions and cloud infrastructure.

## Work Hours and Schedule
- Standard work hours: 9:00 AM - 5:00 PM, Monday to Friday
- Flexible work arrangements available after 6 months
- Remote work: Up to 3 days per week for eligible employees

## Leave Policy
### Paid Time Off (PTO)
- 15 days annually for employees with 0-2 years of service
- 20 days annually for employees with 3-5 years of service
- 25 days annually for employees with 5+ years of service

### Sick Leave
- 10 days of paid sick leave per year
- Additional unpaid sick leave available with medical documentation

## Benefits
- Health insurance coverage (medical, dental, vision)
- 401(k) matching up to 5%
- Annual learning budget: $2,000 per employee
- Gym membership reimbursement: $50/month
- Free lunch on Fridays

## Code of Conduct
All employees must maintain professional behavior, respect diversity, and adhere to our ethics policy.

## Performance Reviews
Performance reviews are conducted bi-annually in June and December.
