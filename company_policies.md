# Company Policies - TechCorp Inc.

## Security Policy

### Password Requirements
- Minimum 12 characters
- Must include uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and symbols
- Cannot reuse last 5 passwords
- Must change every 90 days

### Device Security
- All company devices must use full-disk encryption
- Screen lock required after 5 minutes of inactivity
- Lost devices must be reported within 1 hour
- Personal devices accessing company data must be approved

### Data Classification
- **Public**: Marketing materials, public documentation
- **Internal**: General business information
- **Confidential**: Financial data, customer information
- **Restricted**: Trade secrets, unreleased product info

## Remote Work Policy

### Eligibility
- Must have been employed for at least 6 months
- Role must be suitable for remote work
- Must have reliable internet (minimum 50 Mbps)
- Must have dedicated workspace

### Requirements
- Available during core hours (10 AM - 3 PM local time)
- Respond to messages within 2 hours during work hours
- Attend all scheduled meetings
- Use company-approved VPN for all work activities

## Expense Reimbursement

### Eligible Expenses
- Travel (flights, hotels, rental cars)
- Client meals and entertainment
- Professional development courses
- Office supplies
- Technology equipment (pre-approved)

### Submission Process
1. Submit expense report within 30 days
2. Include itemized receipts
3. Provide business justification
4. Manager approval required for expenses over $500

### Reimbursement Timeline
- Approved expenses paid within 14 business days
- Direct deposit to employee bank account

## Professional Development

### Learning Budget
- $2,000 annually per employee
- Covers courses, certifications, conferences
- Must be job-related
- Requires manager approval

### Conference Attendance
- Up to 2 conferences per year
- Company covers registration, travel, accommodation
- Must present learnings to team afterward
